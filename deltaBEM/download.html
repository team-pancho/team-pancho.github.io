DOWNLOAD!



The deltaBEM basic package contains a collection of uncompiled m.files. Download and decompress the package, put it in the path of Matlab and you are ready to use it. For some plotting routines the PDEToolbox is needed.
Click here to download the package.
Click here to have a look at the documentation (included in the package).
For scripts using the deltaBEM functions, go to Examples.
