Philosophy
This class is reading-and-writing intensive. You'll be asked to read carefully different sections of the book. In class, we'll be working on problems and clarifying concepts, but you will not be getting clean classnotes to work with. (The textbook is where to study from.)
Precision and full rigor in presentation of the proofs is expected of all the students. Most of the problems will be about concepts and proofs, and there'll be very few exercises carrying a lot of computation. For training purposes, you'll be asked to memorize one particular item (most often a proof).
