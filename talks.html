Numerical Analysis & No Regrets

Numerical Analysis & No Negrets conference was held on February 16, 2019 at the Department of Mathematical Sciences at University of Delaware. The conference hosted 23 scholars from various universities in USA and Canada. There were 8 talks on different topics in numerical analysis, and a warm casual dinner at the end of the day.
